Resisting the satanic forces behind COVID 19 plague

1.) Father, by the blood of Jesus, we decree speedy judgement upon the force of hell behind the spread of corona virus thereby eliminating it from the world - *Ex. 12:12/Jud.1:6

2.) Father, in the name of Jesus and by the power of the Holy Spirit, we command the spirit sponsoring corona virus to be swallowed up in victory thereby releasing its victims. - Isa. 59:19 / Isa. 25:8

3.) Father, in the name of Jesus and by the power of the Holy Spirit, we command the spirit of corona virus to be swallowed up in victory thereby liberating the world from bondage - Isa. 59:19/Isa. 25:8

4.) Father, we rain upon all the agents of the devil sponsoring the plague COVID 19 with fire and brimstone thereby silencing them for ever. - Psalm 11:6

5.) Father, we decree enough is enough to corona virus and its scourge in our nation and beyond. Let the spirit of death loose its grip from all affected. 2 Sam 24:16

6.) Father, let there be release of warring angels against any further spread of this plague. Enough is enough in Jesus name. Matt 26:53
