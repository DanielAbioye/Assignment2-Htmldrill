Thanksgiving prayers

1) Father, thank you for gathering great multitudes into our daily prayer session yesterday, thereby exempting us from every plague of this end time – Psa. 118:23 23

2) Father, thank you for your divine protection over the entire winners family and for your preservation from every plague. – Psa. 91:1-2

3) Father, thank you for preservation the body of Christ worldwide from the evil plague ravaging the earth. – Psa. 91:14

4) Father, thank you for supernatural victory over the satanic forces afflicting the nations of the earth with COVID 19. Rev 12:11

5) Father, thank you for the unfailing power of the Covenant resulting to the protection and preservation of the saints. Psa 89:34

6) Father, in the name of Jesus, thank you because your mercy has prevailed over judgement upon the earth and for Liberty from this evil plague. Psa 59:16

7) Father, thank you for marking out a remnant across the nations of earth, and for preserving the saints. Isa 37:32

8) Father, thank you for the dispatch of angelic hosts against this satanic siege and for giving us victory. Psa.103:20-21
